Buffalo Chicken Dip

Mine is better than yours, so enjoy.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredients
1 Cup Diced Chicken
1/4 TSP Salt
1/2 Cup Secret Sauce
1/8 TSP Breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven 350F
  2. Apply secret sauce to chicken
  3. Sprinkle salt & breadcrumbs
  4. Bake for 30 minutes
  5. Enjoy
